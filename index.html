Greetings!

My name is Chris and I am a multifaceted code wrangler and people manager.

What is this place?

This is a loosely tended plot of digital space for some of my thoughts, notes, photographs, regurgitations, and the occasional risky click.

About me

Currently I work as an engineering manager for Muck Rack, a responsive web application that makes it easier for journalists and public relations teams to hone in on the stories that matter most to them. I am grateful for the opportunity to do meaningful work with wonderful people.

When I’m not working you can find me spending time with family and friends, listening to music, exercising, reading, contributing to open-source software, or reminiscing about iconic PC games from the 90’s and 00’s.

Feel free to read my résumé if you are into that sort of thing.
