My Essential Mac OSX Apps

Published on March 14, 2013.

My machines have been feeling a bit sluggish lately, due in part to the amount of applications I’ve downloaded over the past year. The entire Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office, iWork, and a whole mess of smaller applications.
